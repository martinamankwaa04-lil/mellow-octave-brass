Our Story

A Band Built on Passion & Harmony

Founded in Odumase, Sunyani, Mellow Octave Brass began as a small group of music enthusiasts determined to keep the brass band tradition alive in the Brong-Ahafo region. Today, we are one of the most celebrated brass ensembles in the area.

We perform at funerals, festivals, church events, weddings, community celebrations, and cultural parades — bringing the unmistakable warmth of live brass music to every occasion.
